Typography

Design priciples

It is not about chosing fonts, it is to create optimal reading experience.

  • Readable fonts (max of 2 fonts).
  • Only use Siref fonts for reading materials.
  • Easy to read (max of 72 chars per line).
  • Clean and organized.
  • Align texts using baselines.
  • Creates emotional impact.

Colour

Design priciples

Color should be functional, not decorative. Use it to get the attention, stir emotion and suggest associate meaning.

  • Follow standard usage of colors
  • Must provide meaning (color meanings)
  • Must have emotional impact
  • Consider the Color-blinds
  • Only use bright colors to make visual accent
  • Minimize Optical fatigue.

Media

Design priciples

Images can save you lots of words, but it should be meaningful and easy to understand or else it's just visual noise.

  • Should be in high quality resolution
  • Serves a purpose, message, and emotion.
  • Easy to understand.
  • Use icons appropiately (min of 48pixels).
  • Always use popular Icons (add label for uncommon icons).

Imagery

  • Realistic photographs (avoid creapy and fake smiling people).
  • The photographs have tell a story.
  • Focus on people not on things.
  • Use illustration when specificity isn't posible.
